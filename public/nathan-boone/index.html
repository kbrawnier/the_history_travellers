#Manifest Destiny

Nathan Boone

Man of Legends

General Dodge heard the rifles being cocked. He drew his sword and put it 6 inches from Captain Cooper's chest. He let Cooper know in no uncertain terms that if the Captain's men fired upon the prisoners Cooper would be the first to die. Thankfully, that's when Major Nathan Boone came up beside Gen. Dodge and told him that Boone and his men would back the General if worse came to worst. Boone also warned cooper not to do anything stupid.
